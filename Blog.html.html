Certificates & Student's Program

Education pays off. For every year of education completed, projected lifetime income increases. The value of any degree or certificate is based on what you learn — practical knowledge as well as digital skills and soft skills — but employers also like to know that you see commitments through to the end.


Earning a bachelor's degree equates to four years of school and an investment that often surpasses $40,000. While many jobs require a bachelor's degree, certificate programs can equip students with career skills on a tighter timeline — and for a lot less money.


Many online certificate programs are self-paced, which allows students to listen to lectures and complete assignments at their convenience. Because of this flexibility, students can maintain their work obligations while they finish their studies..
